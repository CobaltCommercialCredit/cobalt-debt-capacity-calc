Effective rate is the annualized all-in cost of capital — interest, fees, and (where applicable) amortized origination costs divided by average capital deployed. This is the number to quote a client, not the headline rate, since quoted rates on factoring in particular understate true cost. These two callouts are objective reads of the numbers, not a structuring recommendation — availability and cost are only two of the factors that decide the right instrument.
